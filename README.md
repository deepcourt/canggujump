# 🌴 CANGGU JUMP

### [🎮 PLAY GAME HERE](https://canggujump.carmelospano.com/)

**Built for Pieter Levels' VibeJam 2026**
*Compliance Note: 90%+ of this code was AI-generated using Aider and Gemini.*

Canggu Jump is a high-energy, 8-bit rhythmic runner set in the chaotic streets of Canggu, Bali. Play as a legendary Canggu bodybuilder dodging the everyday chaos of island life.

## 🎮 How to Play
1. **Controls**: Use your **Webcam** (AI Pose Detection) to jump in real life, or use your **Keyboard** if you prefer.
2. **Mechanics**: 
   - Jump over obstacles to survive.
   - **Combo System**: Jump or dodge 3 obstacles in a row to earn back a life (Heart).
   - **Interaction**: Most obstacles can be dodged, but Bali Dogs and Evil Spirits *must* be jumped over.
3. **Survive**: Don't lose all 3 lives!

## 🏃 The Cast
- **💪 The Bodybuilder**: Our hero. Fluo pink shorts, tanned, and ready to jump.
- **🛵 The Scooters**: Solo rider, Grab driver, Surfer with board, and the Family of 3.
- **📸 The Influencer**: Stops in the middle of the road to take photos—careful!
- **🐕 Bali Dogs**: Barking, fast-moving; must be jumped.
- **🎾 Padel Ball**: High-speed projectiles from the local courts.
- **👻 Evil Spirit**: A touch of Balinese bad luck; must be jumped.
- **🥤 Protein Shake**: Invincibility power-up.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **React + Vite**, **MediaPipe Pose**, **Web Audio API**, **Tailwind CSS**.
